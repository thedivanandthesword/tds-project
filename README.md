## 📖 About the Project

**The Divan and the Sword (TDS)** is an independent scholarly research project dedicated to the study of political authority, administrative institutions, governance, and the intellectual heritage of the Iranian and Persianate world.

The project adopts an **evidence-based approach** grounded in transparency, traceability, and scholarly rigor. It integrates historical research, critical source analysis, and digital humanities methods to explore institutional continuity, political thought, and state formation across Iranian history.

---

## 🎯 Research Areas

- Iranian political tradition
- Bureaucracy and administrative history
- Governance and institutional continuity
- Persianate intellectual and cultural history
- Historical source criticism
- Digital humanities
