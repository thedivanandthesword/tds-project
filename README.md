# The Divan and the Sword

**The Divan and the Sword (TDS)** is an independent scholarly research project dedicated to the study of Iranian political tradition, bureaucracy, governance, and the Persianate world through an evidence-based historical methodology.

## Mission

The Divan and the Sword (TDS) seeks to advance rigorous scholarship on Iranian political tradition, bureaucracy, and governance by promoting transparent, evidence-based historical research and openly documented scholarly practices.

## Scope

The Divan and the Sword (TDS) focuses on the historical study of Iranian political tradition, bureaucracy, governance, and the Persianate world. It emphasizes evidence-based scholarship, transparent documentation, and reproducible research practices. It is intended as a historical and scholarly resource and does not seek to advance contemporary policy positions.

## About

This repository contains the project's documentation, metadata, governance framework, and research outputs. It is maintained in accordance with principles of transparency, traceability, objective evidence, and milestone-based scholarly review.
