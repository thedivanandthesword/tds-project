## About the Project

**The Divan and the Sword (TDS)** is an open scholarly research project examining Iranian political tradition, bureaucracy, authority, and the Persianate intellectual heritage.

The project investigates the historical relationship between political authority (*the Sword*) and administrative institutions (*the Divan*), with particular attention to institutional continuity, governance, bureaucratic evolution, and political thought across Iranian history.

All public outputs follow an evidence-based methodology and are designed to remain transparent, reproducible, and citable.

## Scope

The project develops openly accessible scholarly resources, documentation, structured datasets, and digital research tools relating to Iranian political history and administrative traditions.

Its objective is to promote transparent scholarship through reproducible workflows, structured documentation, and stable citation practices.
