# The Divan and the Sword (TDS)

**An independent scholarly research project on Iranian political tradition, bureaucracy, governance, and the Persianate world.**

---

## Welcome

Welcome to the official website of **The Divan and the Sword (TDS)**.

The project is dedicated to the study of political authority, administrative institutions, and the intellectual heritage of the Iranian and Persianate world through rigorous, evidence-based scholarship.

---

## Research Areas

- Iranian political tradition
- Bureaucracy and administrative history
- Governance and institutional continuity
- Persianate intellectual and cultural history
- Historical source criticism
- Digital humanities

---

## Mission

TDS promotes transparent, evidence-based scholarship by integrating historical research, critical source analysis, and digital documentation. The project seeks to advance understanding of governance, political institutions, and statecraft across Iranian history.

---

## Core Principles

- Evidence-based scholarship
- Transparency and traceability
- Scholarly integrity
- Long-term sustainability
- Open documentation
