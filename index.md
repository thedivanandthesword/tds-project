## About the Project

The Divan and the Sword (TDS) is a scholarly research project on Iranian political tradition, bureaucracy, authority, and the Persianate intellectual heritage. It critically examines the dialectical relationship between poetic expression (*Divan*) and socio-political authority (*Sword*), with a focus on institutional continuity, administrative history, and the evolution of governance discourse in the Iranian tradition.

The project follows a rigorous evidence-based methodology, ensuring that all outputs are stable, citable, and publicly accessible under open scholarly standards.

---

## Scope

TDS develops openly accessible scholarly resources, documentation, and research concerning Iranian political thought, administrative history, institutional continuity, and related digital humanities tools. The project prioritizes structured data, reproducible workflows, and transparent citation practices to support future research in the field.
