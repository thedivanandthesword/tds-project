# Release Policy

Each release has a single primary objective.

- **Patch releases** focus on maintenance, documentation, and corrections.
- **Minor releases** introduce new features, design improvements, or usability enhancements.
- **Major releases** introduce substantial architectural or scholarly developments.

All releases follow the principles of:

- Evidence Before Status
- Traceability
- Transparent Documentation
- Open Scholarly Practice
- Reproducible Research

---

# Long-Term Vision

The Divan and the Sword aims to become a transparent, openly documented, and evidence-based scholarly research project dedicated to the study of Iranian political tradition, bureaucracy, governance, and the Persianate world.

Every release is intended to improve either the project's scholarly content, technical infrastructure, or public accessibility while maintaining long-term stability and transparent version control.
